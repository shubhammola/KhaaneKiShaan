Pay Your Bill Here!

Thank you for dining with us at Khaane-ki-Shaan! We appreciate your visit and want to make settling your bill as convenient as possible. Please follow these steps to complete your payment:

  1. Select your respective table number.
  2. Scan the QR code displayed from your mobile.
  3. Please be sure to enter the correct amount or always feel free to take help from our representative.
  4. Choose from our wide variety of payment options, including UPI, Scan and Pay, credit or debit card, net banking, or simply hand over exact cash amount to our representative.
  5. Feel free to tip our staff heartfully. Their dedication and service have been exceptional.
  6. Find details of the honourable chef and the respected waiter served at your table number.
  7. Lastly, please take a moment to rate our service. Your feedback is valuable and helps us improve.

Thank you for giving us the honor of serving you. We look forward to welcoming you back on your next visit! Have a fantastic day, and enjoy your meal!
